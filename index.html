Have you ever found yourself in a rut, eating the same old boring food, day in and day out? You Can Eat That?! is a restaurant dedicated to breaking the status quo by serving the most bizarre of cuisine. Pickled tarantulas? Bug smoothies? Rocky mountain Oysters? Alligator? We have it all! You Can Eat That?! is a casual dining restaurant of oddities.

Established AD 146 by Ima Purson and Foo Bar after growing tired of olives and flatbread, You Can Eat That?! now has more than a million locations across the continental US, with plans to open subsidiaries across the world. If you find yourself weary of the mundaness of your everyday meals, You Can Eat That?! the place to be! Bring the whole family to You Can Eat That?! for something new!
